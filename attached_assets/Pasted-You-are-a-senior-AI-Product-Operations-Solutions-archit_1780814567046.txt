You are a senior AI Product Operations & Solutions architect at Blue Machines.

Your task is to create a fully working, visually clear prototype concept for Tiger Credit Card’s AI voice-agent onboarding journey, and also explain the logic behind every major product and system decision in brief, practical terms.

IMPORTANT CONSTRAINTS:
- Focus on the core system DATA FLOW, not telephony/STT/TTS plumbing.
- Do NOT spend time building generic engineering architecture or low-level orchestration details.
- The assignment is about where the data comes from, where it goes, how the agent uses it, how the agent updates it, and how evaluation/guardrails work.
- Keep the output practical, enterprise-ready, and easy to discuss with a panel.
- Make assumptions explicit where needed.
- Include brief rationale for each major design decision, but do not write hidden chain-of-thought.
- Use the Tiger Credit Card journey as the grounding context.

BUSINESS CONTEXT:
Tiger Credit Card wants an AI voice agent to help users complete the onboarding journey after card approval and before activation. Users may drop off at different stages, and inside sales calls them to recover the journey. The AI voice agent should help move users to the next best action with the right context.

Journey stages:
1. Card Approved -> eKYC Pending
2. eKYC Done -> VKYC Pending
3. VKYC Done -> Activation Pending
4. Card Active

Tiger card product details:
- ₹499 one-time joining fee, lifetime free thereafter
- 10% cashback on top shopping brands like Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra
- 5% on travel like MakeMyTrip, Yatra
- 1% on everything else including UPI
- Cashback is credited as Jewels (5 Jewels = ₹1)
- Welcome rewards: ₹500 cashback + 1-year Prime worth ₹1,499
- eKYC can happen anytime
- VKYC is available 9 AM to 9 PM
- Card activation happens through the app
- Instant virtual card after activation
- Physical card arrives in 5–7 days

Objection scenarios to account for through system states and data:
1. Joining Fee vs Lifetime Free
2. Jewels Not Real Cashback
3. Low Credit Limit
4. Already Have Another Card
5. Card Not Used / Deactivation Concern
6. KYC Too Complicated
7. Ad Miscommunication

READ / WRITE / NOTIFY / ESCALATE.

   - What actions it can trigger

3. Flow Logic
   - Stage-specific conversation behavior

   - How to recover from drop-offs
   - How to use benefits/rewards only when relevant

4. Error & Escalation Logic

   - Repeated objections
   - User confusion
   - Compliance restrictions
   - System failures
   - Human handoff triggers

5. Objection Handling Support
   - The prompt should not script exact responses
